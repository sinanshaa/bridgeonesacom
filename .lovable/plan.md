

# BridgeOne.Com — Premium Corporate Website

## Overview
A modern, premium multi-page corporate website for BridgeOne.Com, an international business services company bridging Saudi Arabia (GCC) and India. The design will reflect a top-tier consulting firm with deep navy, white/grey, and subtle gold accents.

---

## Design & Branding
- **Color Palette:** Deep Navy (#1B2A4A), White (#FFFFFF), Light Grey (#F4F6F8), Gold accent (#C4A35A)
- **Typography:** Poppins for headings, Inter/Open Sans for body text (via Google Fonts)
- **Style:** Clean, minimal, elegant — generous whitespace, smooth scroll animations, subtle fade-ins
- **Fully responsive** across mobile, tablet, and desktop

---

## Global Elements (Present on All Pages)
- **Sticky Navigation Bar** with logo, page links, and a "Free Consultation" CTA button
- **Floating WhatsApp Button** (links to WhatsApp with pre-filled message)
- **Footer** with contact info, quick links, social icons, and "Serving GCC & India" trust badge
- **Free Consultation Popup Form** triggered by CTA buttons (name, email, phone, message)
